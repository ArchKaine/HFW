Key Design Features & Systems (Common HFW Principles)

  • Stealth Integration: Managed by HFW, combines passive signature reduction (e.g., Next-Gen Void Armor derivatives, hull shaping) with active measures (e.g., Adaptive Countershading, Advanced EW Suite, Acoustic Dampening). Relies on careful power management and operational doctrine.
  • Power Systems: Typically feature stealth-grade main and auxiliary power plants ensuring ample power and crucial redundancy.
  • Shield Philosophy: Prioritizes stealth over active shielding during infiltration for most platforms. Stealth-grade shield generators offer protection when activated.
  • 'Ghost Mode' Cockpit & Total Immersion Interior: Advanced Anvil-Esperia display technology projecting 360-degree external views, often extending beyond the bridge.
  • Advanced Materials & Construction: Use of exotic materials, often involving HFW's internal breakthroughs and insights from unconventional research (including alien technology influences like MISC/Mirai's Xi'an tech research ).
