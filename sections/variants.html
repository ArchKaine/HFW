The Spectre Project: Forging the Scalpel

The Spectre was the first major project undertaken by the newly operational Hephaestus Forge Works. Formally commissioned in 2940 by a directive from Imperator Kelos Costigan, it was designed as the UEE's "scalpel" against asymmetric threats. Its core innovation is a multi-role doctrine, deploying four specialized variants as a single, cohesive force.

Spectre Shadow (Stealth/Electronic Warfare)

Role: The premier stealth platform and information warfare hub. Designed for deep infiltration, intelligence gathering, data hacking, and providing an electronic warfare umbrella for other HFW assets.

Description: The Shadow is HFW's truest "ghost." It is designed for persistent, long-duration operations deep within hostile territory. It fights not with overwhelming firepower, but with data, deception, and darkness, turning an enemy's own sensor network against them.

Spectre Strike (Heavy Attack & Ground Support)

Role: A dedicated heavy strike platform that uses stealth to achieve the perfect attack vector before delivering a devastating, overwhelming blow.

Description: The Strike embodies the principle of decisive violence from an unexpected direction. Its stealth systems, while less persistent than the Shadow's, are sufficient to get it into position. Once it opens fire, its objective is total annihilation of the target.

Spectre Lockdown (Quantum Interdiction)

Role: Strategic battlefield control by trapping enemy vessels and preventing quantum travel.

Description: By controlling the flow of battle on a strategic level, the Lockdown creates an inescapable cage in the middle of empty space. It uses its stealth to covertly position itself at a key jump point, waiting for the perfect moment to spring its trap.

Spectre Pulse (Systems Disablement)

Role: HFW's non-lethal solution. The Pulse is designed to disable, disrupt, and neutralize enemy vessels without destroying them, making it the ideal tool for capture, boarding actions, and asset denial.

Description: The Pulse is a master of electronic and energy warfare, but where the Shadow is subtle, the Pulse is a brute. It overwhelms enemy systems with raw power, leaving them dead in the water.

Spectre Revenant (Heavy Interceptor/Light Destroyer)

Classification: Light Destroyer / Heavy Interceptor

Design Philosophy: The Spectre Revenant project was initiated by HFW as a parallel, even more highly classified effort to create the ultimate sentinel for The Forge. It is not merely a variant, but a significant scaling-up of the Spectre's core technologies to a 121-meter frame, designed to neutralize capital-class threats.

General Specifications & Key Features
  • Dimensions: Length: 121m | Beam: 84+m | Height: 20m (23m gear down)
  • Crew Complement: 3–5 (average 3), enabled by advanced HFW automation.
  • Cargo Capacity: ~270 SCU, capable of carrying up to three Ursa rovers.
  • Dual Combat Doctrine: Engineered for both long-range precision sniping and overwhelming close-quarters "knife fights."
The Phoenix Protocol (Pilot Training Program)

Due to its immense power and complexity, pilots for the Revenant are trained using the **"Phoenix Protocol"**—a radical fusion of neural interface technology and a state-of-the-art regeneration facility that allows for consequence-reduced learning from "fatal" combat simulations.

The Anvil Eidolon Series: Agile Shadows for Close Support

While Spectres provide deep infiltration and strategic strikes, the Eidolons fill the crucial role of agile, unseen wingmen, designed to operate seamlessly alongside them and bolster the defenses of The Forge. Uniquely for a fighter, it features a full interior for long-duration missions.

Eidolon Shadow (Stealth Recon & EW Escort)

Role: Advanced stealth reconnaissance, electronic warfare, and dedicated escort. Features a sophisticated, miniaturized Size 2 Electronic Warfare Suite.

Armament: 4x fixed Size 3 Railguns, housed in retractable stealth compartments. Capacity for 4x Size 5 torpedoes in internal bays.

Eidolon Strike (High-Speed Interdiction & Strike Escort)

Role: A dedicated heavy strike fighter blending agility with focused firepower. Features enhanced power distribution for a "Railgun Overcharge" ability.

Armament: 2x fixed Size 4 railguns and 2x fixed Size 3 railguns, all utilizing retractable housings. Capacity for 4x Size 5 torpedoes.

Eidolon Lockdown (Point-Defense & Interdiction Support)

Role: Providing localized quantum enforcement and point defense for capital assets. Equipped with a miniaturized Size 2 QED.

Armament: 4x fixed Size 3 railguns, and 2x Size 1 point defense cannons. Capacity for 4x Size 5 torpedoes.

Eidolon Pulse (EM/EW Suppression & Electronic Cover)

Role: Target disablement via powerful non-lethal EMP and distortion effects. Built around a miniaturized Size 2 EMP Device.

Armament: 2x fixed Size 3 distortion cannons and 2x fixed Size 3 railguns. Capacity for 4x Size 5 torpedoes.

Eidolon Revenant (Heavy Interceptor & Capital Escort)

Role: The ultimate heavy interceptor of the series, designed to support the Spectre Revenant in assaulting capital ships. It hits harder than the Eidolon Strike.

Armament: 2x fixed Size 5 railguns and 2x fixed Size 4 railguns. Capacity for 4x Size 5 torpedoes for anti-capital punch.

The Valkyrie Phantom: Stealthy Insertion

Recognizing the need for covert ground operations, HFW re-engineered the robust Anvil Valkyrie, stripping non-essential components and integrating their own advanced stealth and armament packages internally at The Forge.

Valkyrie Phantom (Base)

Role: Stealth Assault Dropship & Gunship. Delivers 20 operators and an Ursa-class vehicle into contested territory with minimal detection. Retains the Valkyrie's toughness and a significant armament for fire support.

Armament: Features 2x Size 2 pilot guns, two Size 5 remote wing turrets, and two Size 4 top/bottom remote turrets, all retractable. Also retains two Size 1 door guns.

Valkyrie Phantom Pathfinder

Role: Stealthy Reconnaissance and Forward Observation.

Description: This variant precedes the main force, providing clandestine "eyes on target." It sacrifices some troop capacity for an enhanced sensor array, long-range comms, and expanded recon drone capacity.

Valkyrie Phantom Breacher

Role: Covert Entry and Assault Facilitation.

Description: The Breacher is a specialized tool for creating an entrance where none exists. It is tailored for bypassing hardened defenses and is equipped with heavy-duty plasma torches and seismic charges.

Valkyrie Phantom Medical

Role: Combat Search and Rescue (CSAR) and Medical Extraction.

Description: This variant prioritizes casualty collection. Its interior is modified into a mobile trauma bay with Tier 3 medical beds, stasis pods, and specialized extraction equipment like a rescue hoist.

The Pathfinder Program: From Reconnaissance to Retribution

The **Pathfinder MKII** is the direct answer to the lessons learned from the MKI's destruction. It is a total rebuild, engineered to be a **Heavy Reconnaissance Gunship**—a purpose-built hunter.

Pathfinder MKII Scout (Heavy Reconnaissance Gunship)

Role: Long-Range Armed Pathfinder & Strategic Threat Eliminator.

Description: The baseline Pathfinder MKII is designed to perform deep penetration missions, remain completely undetected, and possess overwhelming firepower to neutralize any threat it encounters without an escort. Features the HFW "Umbral Drive" for silent landings and a "WhisperScan" LPI sensor array.

Armament: 2x Size 5 Concealed Railguns and 3x Size 5 Remote Turrets (each with 2x Size 4 Attrition Repeaters).

Pathfinder MKII Surveyor

Role: Stellar Cartography & Covert Scientific Analysis.

Description: This variant sacrifices some combat capability for an enhanced suite of scientific instruments, including geological scanners and an onboard analysis lab. Ideal for covertly assessing resource potential.

Pathfinder MKII Sentinel

Role: Covert Overwatch & Secure Network Relay.

Description: Designed for extremely long-duration static observation. Excels at creating hidden sensor networks and providing a persistent, low-signature C&C node for other HFW assets via an enhanced communications suite.

Daemon: HFW’s Extreme Racing Prototype

HFW’s Extreme Racing Prototype, now in limited production as a “super-scout” racer. Its purpose is to push HFW's expertise in propulsion and low-signature materials into a dedicated speed platform. The result is arguably the fastest and most agile single-seat craft in the UEE. It has no weapon mounts.

HFW Atlas-class (Strategic Hauler)

Classification: Frigate-class / Strategic Heavy Freighter

Role: Strategic Logistics & High-Value Asset Transport. The "Unseen Backbone" of HFW's operations.

Design Philosophy: Engineered with a "Strategic Blockade Runner" philosophy to securely and rapidly move large quantities of sensitive materials. Its primary defenses are its exceptional speed for its size and a suite of signature-reducing technologies. Features a massive forward "Gulp" ramp for vehicle loading.

Capacity & Armament: Carries 7,500 SCU of cargo and is defended by five retractable remote turrets.

HFW Erebus-Prime (Strategic Command Carrier)

Classification: Super-Capital (Flagship)

Role: Clandestine Command & Control, Fleet Carrier, Strategic Strike.

Design Philosophy: The Erebus is not a frontline destroyer; it is a clandestine command carrier. Its power projection comes from its apex stealth systems and its unmatched hangar capacity. It is faster and more maneuverable than any vessel of its size, designed to avoid a direct fight and deploy its own fleet of specialized assets from the shadows.

Armament & Hangar: Its primary weapons are two "Tartarus Reach" Heavy Plasma Lances. It is defended by 4 heavy plasma turrets and a dense point-defense network. It carries a fleet of 24 Eidolons, 4 Valkyrie Phantoms, and 2 Pathfinder MKIIs.
