Select a ship family or a unique capital-class vessel to view its specialized variants developed by Hephaestus Forge Works. Each variant is tailored for specific roles within HFW's covert operations doctrine.

The Anvil Spectre project introduces a new echelon of covert warfare vessels. The operational doctrine emphasizes the coordinated deployment of the Spectre family, allowing them to tackle complex threats through combined arms tactics.

Spectre Shadow (Stealth/Electronic Warfare)

Role: The premier stealth platform and information warfare hub. Designed for deep infiltration, intelligence gathering, data hacking, and providing an electronic warfare umbrella for other HFW assets.

Description: The Shadow is HFW's truest "ghost." It is designed for persistent, long-duration operations deep within hostile territory. It fights not with overwhelming firepower, but with data, deception, and darkness, turning an enemy's own sensor network against them.

Key Features & Systems:

  • Pinnacle Stealth Suite: Features the exclusive Adaptive Countershading system, a sophisticated EW package running on a dedicated Size 2 computer blade, and enhanced acoustic dampening.
  • Data Warfare: Equipped with advanced data-interception tools and remote hacking transmitters.
  • Thermal Obscurant Launchers: Modified turret emplacements deploy "hot smoke," creating a massive thermal bloom to decoy heat-seeking ordnance and break sensor locks.
  • Armament: 2x forward-firing Size 5 Railguns for self-defense or opportunistic strikes.

Spectre Strike (Heavy Attack & Ground Support)

Role: A dedicated heavy strike platform that uses stealth to achieve the perfect attack vector before delivering a devastating, overwhelming blow.

Description: The Strike embodies the principle of decisive violence from an unexpected direction. Its stealth systems, while less persistent than the Shadow's, are sufficient to get it into position. Once it opens fire, its objective is total annihilation of the target.

Key Features & Systems:

  • Overwhelming Firepower: A formidable forward-facing battery of 2x Size 5 and 2x Size 4 Railguns, capable of using specialized HFW Penetrator Munitions.
  • Railgun Overcharge: Experimental capacitors allow for a single, terrifyingly powerful shot that generates immense heat and a massive signature bloom—a "finishing move."
  • Ground Attack Specialist: Equipped with more numerous, fast-tracking Incendiary Turrets for saturating ground targets.

Spectre Lockdown (Quantum Interdiction)

Role: The gatekeeper of the battlefield. The Lockdown's purpose is to manipulate quantum travel, trapping key targets, preventing enemy fleets from escaping, and cutting off reinforcements.

Description: By controlling the flow of battle on a strategic level, the Lockdown creates an inescapable cage in the middle of empty space. It uses its stealth to covertly position itself at a key jump point, waiting for the perfect moment to spring its trap.

Key Features & Systems:

  • Quantum Enforcement Device (QED): Its core is a powerful, long-range Size 3 QED capable of generating a quantum snare large and strong enough to pull multiple ships from quantum travel.
  • Stealth Deployment: Its signature reduction is optimized to mask the massive energy build-up of the QED right up until the moment of activation.
  • Armament: 1x QED (Size 3), supported by 2x forward-firing Size 5 Railguns to engage snared targets.

Spectre Pulse (Systems Disablement)

Role: HFW's non-lethal solution. The Pulse is designed to disable, disrupt, and neutralize enemy vessels without destroying them, making it the ideal tool for capture, boarding actions, and asset denial.

Description: The Pulse is a master of electronic and energy warfare, but where the Shadow is subtle, the Pulse is a brute. It overwhelms enemy systems with raw power, leaving them dead in the water.

Key Features & Systems:

  • EMP Warfare: Built around a powerful, wide-arc Size 3 EMP generator capable of being "focused" for greater range.
  • Distortion & Torpedoes: Features 2x Size 5 Distortion Cannons to strip shields, supported by four integrated launch tubes carrying a payload of 24x Size 5 EMP Torpedoes.
  • Lethal Backup: For targets that must be destroyed, it retains 2x Size 4 Railguns.

The Anvil Eidolon Series: Agile Shadows for Close Support

Following the successful deployment and operational validation of the Spectre series, Hephaestus Forge Works recognized a critical gap in its "shadow fleet." While Spectres excelled at deep infiltration and strategic strikes, they lacked the agility required for close-in escort, rapid interception, and localized point defense.

The Valkyrie Phantom: Stealthy Insertion

Recognizing that even the most potent strike or disabling force requires boots on the ground to secure an objective, HFW developed the Valkyrie Phantom by re-engineering the robust Anvil Valkyrie for covert operations.

Daemon: HFW’s Extreme Racing Prototype

HFW’s Extreme Racing Prototype, now in limited production as a “super-scout” racer—sold under strict contract to military forces and, in civilian trim, to approved private pilots.

Origins & Rationale

Born from an internal challenge inspired by Murray Cup analysis, the Daemon project's goal was to push HFW’s expertise in propulsion and low-signature materials into a dedicated speed platform. The result is arguably the fastest and most agile single-seat craft in the UEE.

HFW Atlas-class (Strategic Hauler)

Classification: Frigate-class / Strategic Heavy Freighter

Role: Strategic Logistics & High-Value Asset Transport

Design Philosophy: The Atlas was engineered with a "Strategic Blockade Runner" philosophy to solve the critical HFW challenge of securely and rapidly moving large quantities of sensitive materials. Its primary defenses are its exceptional speed for its size and a suite of signature-reducing technologies.

General Specifications

  • Length: 220 meters
  • Crew: 4-6
  • Cargo Capacity: 7,500 SCU

Core Systems & Armament

Equipped with dual Size 3 Capital HFW "Helios" series Power Plants and dual Size 3 "Aegis" series Shield Generators. Defensively armed with five retractable remote turrets (two dorsal twin Size 5 laser repeaters, two ventral twin Size 5 laser repeaters, and one rear-facing twin Size 4 ballistic gatling). Hardened with the HFW "Aegis Protocol."

HFW Erebus-Prime (Strategic Stealth Dreadnought)

Classification: Super-Capital (Flagship)

Role: Deep Space Dominance & Mobile Operations Platform

Design Philosophy: The Erebus-Prime is a statement of technological supremacy, designed to orchestrate galaxy-spanning operations from a position of complete obscurity. It is faster and more maneuverable than any vessel of its colossal size.

General Specifications

  • Length: 400 meters
  • Crew: 90 Optimal, 40 Minimum
  • Hangar Complement: 36 Eidolons, 8 Valkyrie Phantoms, & 4 Pathfinders

Key Systems & Armament

The Erebus features the apex "Obsidian Mantle" signature management suite and is protected by dual Size 5 Capital X-Grade "Gorgon's Embrace" shields. Its spinal armament consists of two HFW "Tartarus Reach" Heavy Gravitic Lance Cannons, supported by a devastating array of retractable turrets and strategic ordnance tubes.
