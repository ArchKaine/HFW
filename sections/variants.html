HFW Ship Variants[Back to Top]

Select a ship family or unique asset to view its specialized variants developed by Hephaestus Forge Works. Each is tailored for specific roles within HFW's covert operations doctrine.

The Anvil Spectre project, developed by Hephaestus Forge Works, introduces a new echelon of covert warfare vessels. The operational doctrine emphasizes the coordinated deployment of the Spectre family, allowing them to tackle complex threats through combined arms tactics. While all variants leverage advanced signature reduction, only the Shadow is designed for persistent deep stealth; other variants use moderate stealth for initial positioning before employing high-output systems. All variants are EMP hardened to operate cohesively as a unit.

Spectre Shadow (Stealth/Electronic Warfare)

Role: Advanced stealth infiltration, electronic warfare, intelligence gathering, and signature masking. It is the premier stealth platform of the line.

Description: The ultimate force multiplier, designed by HFW to control the flow of information and support fleet operations from the deepest shadows. It is capable of infiltrating the most heavily defended territories.

Key Features & Systems:

  • Unrivaled Stealth: Represents the pinnacle of HFW's signature management, integrating next-generation Void Armor derivatives with active systems like adaptive countershading, acoustic dampening, and sensor scrambling. Features an electronic 'ghost mode' cockpit operable with armored shutters closed.
  • Dominant EW: Equipped with a powerful electronic warfare suite (dedicated Size 2 component) for jamming, masking friendly vessels, and deploying electronic deception. It mounts an advanced sensor array for intelligence gathering and long-range, secure comms.
  • Data Hacking: Capable of remotely hacking enemy systems.
  • Thermal Obscurant Launchers: Deploys "hot smoke" providing strong visual obscuration and a thermal bloom to break sensor locks and mask escape maneuvers, utilizing modified Incendiary Turret emplacements.
  • Drones: Deploys Reconnaissance, Electronic Warfare, and Data Link Drones.

Armament:

  • 2x forward-firing Size 5 Railguns for opportunistic strikes/defense.
  • Limited Incendiary Turret emplacements, primarily for obscurants.
  • Potential for Anti-Radiation Missiles (ARMs) supported by an integrated autoloader system.

Spectre Strike (Heavy Stealth Attack/Ground Support)

Role: Delivering powerful, targeted strikes against ships and ground installations using stealth for insertion.

Description: A dedicated heavy strike platform blending moderate stealth with devastating pinpoint firepower for surgical assaults.

Key Features & Systems:

  • Stealth: Incorporates moderate signature reduction and masking technology, sufficient for positional advantage before engaging. Activation of primary weapons generates a significant signature bloom.
  • Power/Targeting: Features an enhanced power distribution system and improved targeting sensors.
  • Railgun Overcharge: Specialized capacitors allow a single, high-power shot at the cost of extreme heat, massive signature bloom, and component stress.
  • Ground Attack: Features a more prominent array of Incendiary Turrets for focused damage on ground assets and personnel.
  • Drones: Deploys Targeting and Defense Suppression Drones.

Armament:

  • 2x Size 5 forward-firing Railguns.
  • 2x Size 4 forward-firing Railguns.
  • Specialized Penetrator Munitions are available.

Spectre Lockdown (Interdiction/Quantum Enforcement)

Role: Quantum Enforcement and Interdiction.

Description: Offers unique strategic advantage by controlling quantum travel, trapping enemies and setting the stage for coordinated attacks.

Key Features & Systems:

  • Quantum Enforcement: Equipped with a Quantum Enforcement Device (QED) with snare capability, likely a Size 3 component.
  • Stealth: Features good signature reduction capabilities allowing for covert QED deployment, though its baseline signature is higher than the Shadow. QED activation creates a substantial, temporary signature.

Armament:

  • 1x Quantum Enforcement Device.
  • 2x forward-firing Size 5 Railguns.
  • Potential for Anti-Radiation Missiles (ARMs) supported by an integrated autoloader system.
  • (No Incendiary Turrets).

Spectre Pulse (EMP/Electronic Warfare)

Role: Electronic Warfare and Target Disablement via EMP and Distortion weapons.

Description: Provides powerful non-lethal options for disabling enemy vessels from a distance, while retaining lethal backup capabilities.

Key Features & Systems:

  • EMP Warfare: Features a powerful EMP device (Likely a Size 3 component) capable of synchronized output. Systems are EMP hardened.
  • Stealth: Moderate signature reduction focused on minimizing detection prior to system use; has a higher baseline signature than the Shadow. Weapon and torpedo use generates significant signatures.

Armament:

  • 2x Size 5 Distortion Cannons.
  • 4x Size 5 EMP Torpedo Launch Ports (24 torpedoes total) with integrated autoloading system.
  • 2x Size 4 Railguns for lethal backup.
  • (No Incendiary Turrets).

Spectre Revenant (Heavy Interceptor/Light Destroyer)

Initiated by HFW as the ultimate sentinel for The Forge's perimeter, it leverages Spectre advancements scaled for heavy defense and anti-capital capability. It represents HFW's triumph in achieving Shadow-class stealth on a platform of its unprecedented size (122m).

Role: Heavy Interceptor/Light Destroyer. Its primary role is the main line of defense for the HFW base, eliminating capital ships, with a secondary role of super heavy fire support away from base.

Key Design Features & Systems:

  • Dual Combat Doctrine: Its design supports both long-range engagement using forward armament and close-quarters combat. It is fully capable of going into 'knife-fight' range and coming out unscathed.
  • Transport Capacity: It has capacity for 270 SCU of cargo and is capable of transporting up to three Ursa rovers.
  • Crew: Operates with a crew of 3-5 (average 3), utilizing streamlined control systems.

Armament:

  • Forward-Facing: 4x Size 6 Railguns and 4x Size 5 Railguns.
  • Turrets: 6x Quad-mounted turrets, each mounting Size 5 Railguns (24 total).
  • Point Defense: 8x Dual Point Defense Cannons (Size 3), totaling 16 PDCs.

Pilot Training Program (Phoenix Protocol)

Operating the Spectre Revenant requires mastery of its dual doctrine, for which conventional training is insufficient. To cultivate these elite pilots, HFW initiated the "Phoenix Protocol". This is not merely simulation; it's a radical fusion of neural interface technology and a state-of-the-art regeneration facility that creates an environment indistinguishable from reality for the participant. The process allows pilots to experience and learn from catastrophic failures as if they were real, without the prohibitive cost of permanent death or long-term degradation.

The Anvil Eidolon Series: Agile Shadows for Close Support

Following the successful deployment of the Spectre series, HFW recognized a critical gap for an agile escort and rapid interceptor. HFW engineers were tasked with scaling down Spectre technologies into a compact, single-pilot fighter frame to create the Eidolon: the invisible hand supporting the invisible hammer.

A. General Specifications & Key Features (Base Eidolon)

  • Dimensions: Length: 33.7m, Width: 31.4m, Height: 7m.
  • Full Interior: Designed for long-duration covert operations, featuring a bed, food prep station, 2-4 SCU storage, shower/toilet facility, and a suit locker.
  • Armament Philosophy: All weapons are retractable or concealed. All variants have at least four fixed weapons and can carry at least 4x Size 5 torpedoes. All variants are Anti-Radiation Missile (ARM) capable.
  • Escape Enhancement: All variants feature a miniaturized Thermal Obscurant Launcher.

Eidolon Shadow (Stealth Recon & EW Escort)

Role: The premier stealth variant of the line, for scouting, electronic warfare, and providing an EW screen for larger ships.

Key Features: Features a sophisticated, miniaturized Size 2 Electronic Warfare Suite.

Armament: 4x fixed Size 3 Railguns and capacity for 4x Size 5 Torpedoes.

Eidolon Strike (High-Speed Interdiction & Strike Escort)

Role: A heavy strike fighter blending agility with focused firepower for rapid, targeted strikes.

Key Features: Features enhanced power distribution for a "Railgun Overcharge" ability.

Armament: 2x fixed Size 4 Railguns, 2x fixed Size 3 Railguns, and capacity for 4x Size 5 Torpedoes.

Eidolon Lockdown (Point-Defense & Interdiction Support)

Role: Providing localized quantum enforcement and point defense for capital assets.

Key Features: Equipped with a miniaturized Size 2 Quantum Enforcement Device (QED) with limited snare capability.

Armament: 4x fixed Size 3 Railguns, 2x Size 1 Point Defense Cannons, and capacity for 4x Size 5 Torpedoes.

Eidolon Pulse (EM/EW Suppression & Electronic Cover)

Role: Target disablement via powerful non-lethal EMP and distortion effects.

Key Features: Built around a miniaturized Size 2 EMP Device.

Armament: 2x fixed Size 3 Distortion Cannons, 2x fixed Size 3 Railguns, and capacity for 4x Size 5 Torpedoes.

Eidolon Revenant (Heavy Interceptor & Capital Escort)

Role: The ultimate heavy interceptor of the series, designed to support the Spectre Revenant against capital-grade threats. It hits harder than the Eidolon Strike.

Key Features: Achieves Shadow-class stealth at a fighter scale and features enhanced power and cooling for sustained combat.

Armament: 2x fixed Size 5 Railguns, 2x fixed Size 4 Railguns, and capacity for 4x Size 5 Torpedoes.

The Anvil Valkyrie Phantom: Stealthy Insertion

The Valkyrie Phantom was born from the necessity to insert and extract ground personnel and assets without compromising stealth. HFW adopted the proven Valkyrie frame, stripped non-essential components, and performed all subsequent modification and production internally at The Forge.

Anvil Valkyrie Phantom (Base)

Role: A Stealth Assault Dropship and Special Operations Transport designed to deliver a full contingent of troops (20 operators) and a ground vehicle (up to Ursa Rover size) into hostile territory with a minimal chance of detection.

Armament:

  • Pilot Fixed Guns: 2x Size 2 Hardpoints in retractable housings.
  • Remote Wing Turrets: 2x Size 5 Remote Turrets in retractable stealth pods, typically with Size 4 repeaters.
  • Retractable Remote Turrets (Top/Bottom): 2x Size 4 Retractable Remote Turrets, each mounting 2x Size 3 guns, replacing the original manned turrets.
  • Missiles: All standard missile hardpoints have been removed by HFW to prioritize a low-observable profile.

Valkyrie Phantom Pathfinder

Role: Stealthy Reconnaissance and Forward Observation.

Description: This variant is designed to precede the main force, gathering critical intelligence. It sacrifices some troop/storage space for an enhanced sensor array, long-range communication systems, and an expanded capacity for deployable reconnaissance drones.

Valkyrie Phantom Breacher

Role: Covert Entry and Assault Facilitation.

Description: Tailored for bypassing hardened defenses, its internal space is reconfigured to carry specialized breaching equipment, heavy explosive charges, and automated defense suppression systems.

Valkyrie Phantom Medical

Role: Combat Search and Rescue (CSAR) and Medical Extraction.

Description: This variant prioritizes casualty collection and medical care in hot zones. Its interior is modified into a mobile trauma bay with advanced medical facilities and specialized extraction equipment.

The Anvil Pathfinder MKII: Charting the Unseen

The loss of the Pathfinder MKI during "Operation: Ember Silence" confirmed that retrofitted stealth on conventional frames was insufficient for high-risk environments. This validated the immediate need for the Pathfinder MKII program, which must incorporate a purpose-built stealth frame, silent propulsion via the "Umbral Drive," and significant defensive armament.

Pathfinder MKII (Deep Reconnaissance & Intelligence)

Role: A dedicated long-range reconnaissance, exploration, and clandestine pathfinding platform designed to operate autonomously for extended periods deep behind enemy lines or in uncharted space. It is one of the unseen eyes of HFW operations.

Key Features: Emphasizes its advanced HFW "WhisperScan" passive sensor suite and SIGINT capabilities. It utilizes the HFW "Umbral Drive" for ultra-low signature maneuvering and landings.

Armament: Primarily defensive, with retractable armaments for disabling pursuit. As a dedicated scout, it is not intended for direct combat engagements.

HFW Thanatos-class Guardian (Definitive Edition)

Classification: Dreadnought-Scale Assault Platform / Apex Predator

Role: Apex-tier fleet guardian and offensive vanguard for the HFW Erebus-Prime. Its primary function is to establish absolute battlespace dominance and annihilate any threat to the flagship. In secondary capacities, it serves as a long-range, multi-role command platform and experimental weapons testbed.

Design Philosophy & Rationale

The Thanatos embodies the HFW doctrine of the intelligent application of overwhelming power. Its draconic silhouette is not a product of aesthetic mimicry but of convergent tactical evolution; HFW's most advanced combat simulations concluded this wide, bladed form was the single most stable and effective weapons platform for its mass and singular role. It is a work of art sculpted for war, designed to inspire awe and terror in equal measure.

Color Scheme

The Thanatos is presented in the HFW command livery: a deep, non-reflective carbon-black composite hull accented with sharp, aggressive lines of crimson red. These accents are integrated into the ship's thermal regulation network, glowing with intense energy during weapon-charging cycles.

Backstory & Lore: The Nemesis Protocol

The conceptual genesis of the Thanatos can be traced to the aftermath of Operation: Ember Silence in 2945, which proved that even HFW's most valuable assets were vulnerable. Dr. Thorne initiated the top-secret "Project Nemesis" with a single directive: create an insurance policy for the HFW Erebus, which was commissioned c. 2954-2955.

Constructed in total secrecy within The Forge, its existence was known only to Thorne's inner circle. The crew, known as the "Wardens," are HFW's most disciplined veterans, trained to act as a seamless extension of the flagship's will. To the rest of the galaxy, the Thanatos is a myth. Its operational doctrine ensures there are no witnesses.

To those who see this ship, a warning. If you see her, she sees you and is awaiting your surrender. Failure to surrender will result in your deletion. Fail to heed this warning at your peril, for you are in mortal danger. Thanatos has come.

Standard HFW Features

  • HFW "Aegis Protocol" System Hardening: Exceptionally resilient to EMP, distortion, and other energy-based warfare.
  • "Umbral Leviathan" Gravitic Drive Array: Utilizes a colossal network of high-output gravitic repulsors for near-silent VTOL and atmospheric flight, replacing traditional landing gear.
  • "Ghost Mode" Command Bridge: A heavily armored, windowless citadel providing complete, 360-degree situational awareness via projected displays.

Core Systems & Components

The component loadout is engineered for total combat dominance and unprecedented resilience. The staggering power output from the five "Ares" Combat Cores is necessary to run its immense weapon arrays and, most critically, its six "Gorgon's Aegis" shield generators. This massive shield array allows the Thanatos to withstand several direct hits from Size 10 capital-class weaponry and remain operational, a level of durability unmatched by any other ship in its class.

The Daemon Project: The Apex of Speed

Born from an internal challenge inspired by Murray Cup analysis, the Daemon Project's goal was to push HFW's expertise in propulsion and low-signature materials into a dedicated speed platform. While marketed as a racer, its military applications as a "super-scout" for rapid reconnaissance are its primary, if unstated, purpose. The Daemon and Eidolon development programs were greenlit in 2942.

Daemon (Racing & Super-Scout)

Role: Experimental Racing & Super-Scout Vessel.

Key Features:

  • Signature Management: While not a true stealth ship, its hull is composed of naturally low-EM composite panels and passive IR diffusion meshes.
  • System Hardening: Core systems are exceptionally resilient to EMP and distortion damage via the HFW "Aegis Protocol".
  • Safety: The design has no weapon mounts and includes a mandatory HFW flight training program and an advanced ejection pod.

'Nightshade' (Experimental Super-Scout / Interceptor)

Role: A one-of-a-kind discretionary deep-penetration assetand a personal gift from Dr. Aris Thorne to Jax Rylan. It fuses the Daemon's racing performance with next-generation stealth and the teeth of a heavy fighter.

Key Experimental Systems:

  • "Nocturne-Zero" Ablative Hull Coating: An experimental coating that actively absorbs sensor energy, resulting in an incredibly low base signature.
  • HFW "Styx" System: A power-intensive system that temporarily "sinks" all emissions and scrambles targeting locks, making the ship a sensor ghost.
  • Extreme Cooling: The cargo bay is repurposed to house two oversized Size 3 HFW "Cryo-Core" Coolers to manage heat from the 'Styx' system.
  • Advanced Systems: Features a miniaturized "Argus-Mini" strategic sensor suite and an experimental Size 2 "Aether Core" reactor.

Armament:

  • Concealed Main Guns: 6x Size 2 Concealed HFW "Specter" Railguns, housed behind signature-dampened, quick-deploy panels.

HFW Erebus-Prime (Strategic Stealth Dreadnought)

The Erebus-Prime is not merely a ship; it is a statement of technological supremacy and the absolute zenith of HFW's mastery over signature reduction and sustained covert warfare at a scale previously thought impossible. Its design philosophy is one of absolute covert supremacy and enduring operational autonomy, projecting power through undetectable presence and information dominance. The first hull, HFW Erebus, was commissioned c. 2955.

General Specifications:

  • Length: 400 meters
  • Beam (Width): 288 meters
  • Crew Complement: 90 Optimal (Command & Operations Staff) | 40 Minimum
  • Mission Complement: Capacity for up to 160 additional personnel (Ghost Teams, etc.)
  • Cargo Capacity: 6,000 SCU (in dedicated, shielded holds)

Key Design Features & Systems:

  • "Obsidian Mantle" Apex Signature Management Suite: A synthesis of HFW's most advanced stealth technologies, including a "Nocturne Weave" Adaptive Hull and a "Night's Veil" Full-Spectrum Chameleon System.
  • HFW "Phantom Echo" Ghost Field Emitter: An advanced EW system that projects a complex cloud of false sensor returns to make precise targeting extremely challenging.
  • "Cryo-Static" Thermal Regulation Network: An extreme cooling system that allows the ship to operate in a "Sub-Kelvin" emission state for extended periods by shunting heat into massive cryo-sinks.
  • "Nyx-Eye" Fleet Command Nexus (FCN): An expansive, multi-deck, heavily fortified command center for orchestrating galaxy-spanning operations.
  • "Umbral Leviathan" Gravitic Drive Array: A colossal network of gravitic repulsors providing near-silent VTOL and atmospheric flight.

Armament & Defensive Systems (All Fully Retractable):

  • Primary Spinal Armament: 2x HFW "Tartarus Reach" Heavy Gravitic Lance Cannons.
  • Heavy Retractable Turrets: 10x Twin-linked Size 7 Heavy Plasma Accelerator Cannon Turrets.
  • Broadside Emitter Arrays: 8x Size 6 Heavy Sustained Laser Emitter Arrays.
  • Strategic Ordnance Delivery System: 12x Size 10 Heavy Torpedo / Capital Cruise Missile Tubes (48 total ordnance).

Hangar & Support Craft Complement:

  • Stealth Fighter Wing: Up to 36 HFW Eidolon-class multi-role stealth fighters.
  • Support & Assault Craft: Up to 8 HFW Valkyrie Phantom dropships/gunships.
  • Reconnaissance & Command Support: Up to 4 HFW Pathfinder MKII deep recon vessels.
